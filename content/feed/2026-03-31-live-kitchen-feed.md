For the last 3–4 years, most of my meals have come from Zomato or Swiggy.

And honestly, I’m pretty sure the food is often prepared in ways I wouldn’t want to see.

That got me thinking:

What if customers could watch their food being cooked and packed live?

Not polished marketing videos.
Just a simple live feed from the kitchen when your order is being prepared.

With today’s tech, this is actually quite easy to build — and it doesn’t even require expensive cloud infrastructure.
